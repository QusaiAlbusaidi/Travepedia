OUR STORY

Discover the passion behind Travepedia

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate students of Sultan Qaboos University with an uncompromising love for travelling and exploration. What began as campus debates about our dream destinations soon evolved into Travepedia - a website focused on bringing the life-changing experience of travel to others.

Our Mission

As adventurous students of Sultan Qaboos University, we craft authentic travel experiences based on both our extensive local knowledge and global discoveries. Having discovered Oman's hidden gems, from mountain trails we walk between lectures to hidden cafes where we study - we now share these insider tips while integrating lessons from our own student travels across Asia, Europe and beyond. We craft experiences that blend profound intimacy with Omani culture and the spirit of adventure of a young traveler, making travel into value conscious, knowledge-rich explorations that inspire as much as they excite.

Our Team

Our team is made up of SQU students, passionate travelers who combine our academic knowledge with real-world adventure experience. .
